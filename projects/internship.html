Internship at Vleppo

While I was studying at SDU I had the pleasure of getting a 6 month internship at Vleppo. Vleppo is a Nordic Tech company based in Odense (How convinent for me). Vleppo works with a lot of cool stuff like robotics, data analytics and machine learning but my internship focused mostly on Vleppo's blockchain and automating some tasks with python.

Starting out

I jumped into this internship with about the same knowledge of blockchain as what a quick google search and 10 minutes on wikipedia will give you. Knowing that the blockchain would be written in C and me also not having ANY experience programming C I knew I was in for a ride.

I lied

I actually didn't start programming the blockchain with C right away. I first got the task of automating boring tasks for an SMTP server. This was written in Python which I actually knew how to program in. On a serious note, if you're taking up programming as a hobby or just want to be able to set up a quick project, Python is a great language to start at. Vleppo being a smaller startup didn't really have a huge development team which gave me a lot of freedom/responsibility to develop this any way I wanted. This was great as it was my first time working full time I basically got a list of requirements and was left alone. This might sound like though love but it worked for me because I knew Python. Thank god they didn't do it this way when I had to learn blockchain.

Blockchain

Now as previously mentioned I had no previous experience/knowledge of blockchain. The interview was basically "Hey we need people. Are you interested?" and I was like "Yeah you guys seem awesome". Spoiler alert: They were awesome. I got a designated mentor. Let's call him Dan, cause that was what we called him. Super smart, nice, chill guy and lucky for me very patient. You see blockchain isn't exactly simple. It's actually quite complicated but dare I say I picked it up relatively fast and began wokring on the actual blockchain and not just reading old documentation and bugging Dan.

Thanks man!

To end this off, my 6 months at Vleppo were amazing and I want to thank Vleppo for being an amazing first experience at working full time and for teaching me so much. Especially you Dan. You're amazing! You too Jesper ;)
